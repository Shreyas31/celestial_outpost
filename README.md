##Celestial Outpost
An interactive web application designed for a community of night-sky wanderers to document and share their astronomical findings. Whether you are a beginner or a veteran stargazer, this platform serves as a shared frontier to chart sightings, compare notes, and explore the cosmos collectively.

##Features
Observation Journal: Log your cosmic moments by recording the star observed, the telescope used, and the specific location (city and country).

Dynamic Star Catalog: Linked directly to the SIMBAD astronomical database, providing immediate access to rich data for any star queried.

Telescope Database: A comprehensive record of various instruments, detailing their aperture, focal length, and limiting magnitude to help users understand equipment performance.

Stargazer Community: Connect with fellow explorers, view "Community Champions" with the most contributions, and browse a history of observations from other members.

NASA Integration: The home page features a dynamic background that refreshes daily using the NASA Image of the Day API.

##Tech Stack
Framework: Flask (Python)

Database: PostgreSQL with SQLAlchemy ORM

Astronomical Data: Astroquery (SIMBAD)

Styling: HTML5, CSS3, and Jinja2 templates
